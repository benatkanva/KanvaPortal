## How to Add to Settings

1. Go to http://localhost:3001/settings
2. For each rep in the "Sales Team Roster", add their Fishbowl username to the "Sales Person" field
3. Mark commissioned reps as "Active"
4. Mark non-commissioned staff as "Inactive" or don't add them
